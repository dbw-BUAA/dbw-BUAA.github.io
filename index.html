Brief Biography

    Prof.Du is with the State Key Laboratory of Software Development Environment, Beihang University, IEEE computational Intelligence Society TC Member, Young Expert Committee of Rail transport branch of China Civil Engineering Society and High - Speed Railway Commission in China Railway Society.

    Research interests include smart city technology, multi-source data fusion, and traffic data mining. Received the PhD. degree in computer science and engineering from Beihang University, Beijing, China, in 2013. From 2015 to 2016, worked as a visiting scholar in Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey.

    Prof.Du has presided over many research projects including Natural Science Foundation of China, National Key R&D Program of China etc. More than 100 peer-reviewed journal (e.g., TKDE, TITS) and conference papers (e.g., KDD, AAAI, SIGIR) were published online. Numerous honors, including one second prize of National Science and Technological Invention Award, one second prize of National Scientific and Technological Progress Award, three first prizes of Provincial and Ministerial Science and Technology Award and two second prizes of Provincial and Ministerial Science and Technology Award were received.

    Also serves as an Editorial Board of Intelligent Transportation Infrastructure (2022-2023), PC member of IJCAI, KDD, AAAI, etc. Reviewer for TKDE, TNNLS, TITS, etc.
